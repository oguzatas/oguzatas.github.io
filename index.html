About THFA

Preserving the martial heritage of Europe

Our Mission

The Traditional Historical Fencing Academy is dedicated to the study, practice, and preservation of Historical European Martial Arts (HEMA). We focus on authentic interpretation of historical manuscripts and treatises to bring these ancient fighting systems back to life.

What is HEMA?

Historical European Martial Arts encompasses the study of martial arts practiced in Europe from the 14th to 18th centuries. Using historical manuscripts and treatises, we reconstruct and practice authentic techniques with period-appropriate weapons including longswords, rapiers, daggers, and polearms.
